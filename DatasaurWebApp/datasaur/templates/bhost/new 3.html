How It Works

Pre-Order Now
  1. NOMAD DEVICE - Stays on your pet's collar and tracks your pet's direction and activity using an advanced algorithm that we the PawPrint team has developed.  
  2. NOMAD DEVICE - Stays on your pet's collar and tracks your pet's direction and activity using an advanced algorithm that we the PawPrint team has developed.  
  3. MOBILE CONNECTION - Your mobile device will not only show you your pet's location in relation to yours on a Google Map, it will also convey how your pet reached that location using a special idea the PawPrint team designed of following your pet's movements with PawPrints! 
  4. RANGE - Because our technology works using a two-way radio concept, there is a range for tracking your pet. That range is dependent on line-of-site. But don't worry, even if your pet has gone out of range, you will still be able to see where the PawPrints have gone. Follow the pawprints, and when your pet comes back into range, the signal will continue to send from the Nomad to the Shepherd, and portray the data on your mobile device.
